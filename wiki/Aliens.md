## Anal probes

> Okay, so dogs do it. Dogs go up their butthole. ... What would be interesting is if the aliens had some of these dog elements to them, and you greet them, and the first thing they want to do is go around and sniff your butt.

## Aliens would look more different from us than plants do

Neil deGrasse Tyson explained that since we're even further separated from aliens than from plants on a phylogenetic tree, we should expect them to look nothing like us.

> If they did bring out the alien, it would be almost anticlimactic. Given what's been described about it, and given how we've been treated to every possible imagined alien that you can come up with in a century of storytelling on that subject. So you know what would surprise me? If they did bring out the alien and it was humanoid, because that's weird, most life on Earth does not look human with whom we have DNA in common. We have 20-25% identical genes with a banana. So an alien from another galaxy that has no genes at all should look at least as different from us as we and bananas look from each other.
