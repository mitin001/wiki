Reverse osmosis — a water filtration technique that can remove the most consequential contaminants found in the drinking tap water: [disinfection byproducts] and PFAS. In contrast, charcoal filtration can only remove disinfection byproducts but not PFAS.

[disinfection byproducts]: https://ntp.niehs.nih.gov/research/assessments/cancer/completed/haa
